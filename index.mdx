Welcome to **יסודות פון אידישער גראמאטיק** (Foundations of Yiddish Grammar), a comprehensive reference handbook for Yiddish writers, editors, and proofreaders. This handbook covers the essential grammatical rules and spelling conventions of Yiddish, making it easier to write and proofread with consistency and clarity.
